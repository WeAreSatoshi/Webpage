Coin Swap Instructions

Important Notes:
Note 1: Do not attempt to swap WAS v1 to v2 by withdrawing from an exchange or online wallet, you must be using the 0.8.99.9 software wallet!
Note 2: If the amount of WAS v1 or the amount of WAS v2 that would be issued is less than the coin TX fee * 2 (0.0002 in both cases) then it will be ignored by the swap software.
Note 3: If you forget to enter a WAS v2 address or enter an invalid address (such as accidentally entering a WAS v1 address) your coins will be returned to you automatically.

Swap Directions:
Step 1: Open your WAS v1 client and select the Send tab.
Step 2: Put your new WAS v2 address in the 'Transaction comment' box. [You can find this in your WAS v2 client.]
Step 3: Put WQ3QSkqNiXGzu6xLSaeujccRRcUaPtEwxx in the 'Pay To' box.
Step 4: Put the amount you want to swap in the 'Amount' box.
Step 5: Click the Send button.
Step 6: Wait for the coins to get 10 confirms, you should receive your new WAS v2 within a few minutes at a 1000:1 ratio.
