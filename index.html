WeAreSatoshi Coin

WeAreSatoshi, or WAS, is a peer to peer cryptocurrency designed to remind us all that any one of us has the potential to change the world. The coin is designed to be fast, adaptable, and with the goal of mass adoption to the mainstream market.

The community has developed a faucet with enough coins to provide for ~15000 individuals!

WeAreSatoshi (WAS) Features:
SHA-256D Algorithm
30 Second Block Target
Fast Difficulty Adjustments, designed to limit the impact of multipool mining.
100,000 WAS Reward, halving every 200,000 blocks. Never dropping below 1000 WAS.
