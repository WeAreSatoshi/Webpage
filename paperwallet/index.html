A WeAreSatoshi wallet is as simple as a single pairing of a WeAreSatoshi address with it's corresponding WeAreSatoshi private key. Such a wallet has been generated for you in your web browser and is displayed above.

To safeguard this wallet you must print or otherwise record the WeAreSatoshi address and private key. It is important to make a backup copy of the private key and store it in a safe location. This site does not have knowledge of your private key. If you are familiar with PGP you can download this all-in-one HTML page and check that you have an authentic version from the author of this site by matching the SHA1 hash of this HTML with the SHA1 hash available in the signed version history document linked on the footer of this site. If you leave/refresh the site or press the "Generate New Address" button then a new private key will be generated and the previously displayed private key will not be retrievable. Your WeAreSatoshi private key should be kept a secret. Whomever you share the private key with has access to spend all the WeAreSatoshi associated with that address. If you print your wallet then store it in a zip lock bag to keep it safe from water. Treat a paper wallet like cash.

Add funds to this wallet by instructing others to send WeAreSatoshi to your WeAreSatoshi address.

Check your balance by going to a block explorer and entering your WeAreSatoshi address.

Spend your WeAreSatoshi by downloading the WeAreSatoshi wallet and importing your private key. Keep in mind when you import your single key to a WeAreSatoshi p2p client and spend funds your key will be bundled with other private keys in the p2p client wallet. When you perform a transaction your change will be sent to another WeAreSatoshi address within the p2p client wallet. You must then backup the p2p client wallet and keep it safe as your remaining WeAreSatoshi will be stored there. Satoshi advised that one should never delete a wallet.

Why should I use a Bulk Wallet to accept WeAreSatoshi on my website?
The traditional approach to accepting WeAreSatoshi on your website requires that you install the official WeAreSatoshi client daemon ("WeAreSatoshid"). Many website hosting packages don't support installing the WeAreSatoshi daemon. Also, running the WeAreSatoshi daemon on your web server means your private keys are hosted on the server and could get stolen if your web server is hacked. When using a Bulk Wallet you can upload only the WeAreSatoshi addresses and not the private keys to your web server. Then you don't have to worry about your WeAreSatoshi wallet being stolen if your web server is hacked.
How do I use a Bulk Wallet to accept WeAreSatoshi on my website?
  1. Use the Bulk Wallet tab to pre-generate a large number of WeAreSatoshi addresses (10,000+). Copy and paste the generated comma separated values (CSV) list to a secure text file on your computer. Backup the file you just created to a secure location.
  2. Import the WeAreSatoshi addresses into a database table on your web server. (Don't put the wallet/private keys on your web server, otherwise you risk hackers stealing your coins. Just the WeAreSatoshi addresses as they will be shown to customers.)
  3. Provide an option on your website's shopping cart for your customer to pay in WeAreSatoshi. When the customer chooses to pay in WeAreSatoshi you will then display one of the addresses from your database to the customer as his "payment address" and save it with his shopping cart order.
  4. You now need to be notified when the payment arrives. Google "WeAreSatoshi payment notification" and subscribe to at least one WeAreSatoshi payment notification service. There are various services that will notify you via Web Services, API, SMS, Email, etc. Once you receive this notification, which could be programmatically automated, you can process the customer's order. To manually check if a payment has arrived you can use Block Explorer. Replace THEADDRESSGOESHERE with the WeAreSatoshi address you are checking. It could take between 10 minutes to one hour for the transaction to be confirmed.
    http://www.blockexplorer.com/address/THEADDRESSGOESHERE

    Unconfirmed transactions can be viewed at: http://blockchain.info/
    You should see the transaction there within 30 seconds.
  5. WeAreSatoshi will safely pile up on the block chain. Use the original wallet file you generated in step 1 to spend them.

The WeAreSatoshi Development Team is proud to provide you with a paper wallet.
Please select a category of Paper Wallet from the above menu, and follow the instructions outlined within. You can begin sending WeAreSatoshi to the public address without any additional steps. Although, we recommend sending a small test transactions to ensure the system is functioning correctly.

To collect WeAreSatoshi off of a Paper Wallet, you must enter the command line of the QT client or use WeAreSatoshid. You must unlock your wallet to import a private key, then on the command line enter "importprivkey [private key] [Wallet Label]" and press enter. Your computer will use the private key to unlock your Paper Wallet and include it in your Hot Wallet.

Note: Unless you are comfortable with BIP38 and other encryption techniques, you should avoid those options. If you send WeAreSatoshi to a wallet that you can not decrypt or unlock, those WeAreSatoshi are gone forever!
